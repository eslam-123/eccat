Brief about ECCAT :

The College was established in 2018. It is the first college in Egypt as a model for cooperation between the civil society represented by Misr Al-Kheir Foundation and a foreign partner, Beijing Information Technology College (BITC) with Suez Canal University. The College is working to maximize the role of technical and technological education in the service of industry and national economy to keep up with the rapid development in all technological sciences. The ECCAT also seeks to graduate technical cadres trained on the latest technological applications to meet the needs for development projects in and out the Suez Canal area.

Departments :

Communication Technology:

Mobile network maintenance companies, Programming companies, training companies in the preparation of servers and surveillance devices, Satellite stations, Electricity companies.

Mechatronics Technology:

Electric and car assembly companies, Petroleum drilling companies and data collection and analysis, Medical equipment maintenance companies, Industrial control operations and operation of production lines, Manufacturers of electrical components and materials detectors.

Electronics Technology:

Companies for the assembly and manufacture of radio and television, Manufacturers of electrical lighting components, Auto assembly and manufacturing companies, Textile and medical fabrication companies.
